Welcome to Frankston Chess Club. Our Club has returned to life after the lockdowns of 2020, with a solid core of members, financial stability and a happy atmosphere on Wednesday evenings. Our Members are invited to use our website to keep abreast of activities at the Club. New chess players and backgammon players in the Frankston area are warmly invited to come down and join us at Leawarra House on Wednesday evenings. Many people have taken up playing online chess during the lockdown period, so why not come along and play some face to face chess too!
