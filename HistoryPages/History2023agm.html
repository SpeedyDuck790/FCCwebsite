Chess History EXAMPLE PAGE

Origins of Chess

Chess is believed to have originated in India during the Gupta Empire in the 6th century. It was known as "Chaturanga" and was played on an 8x8 board.

Spread of Chess

Chess spread to Persia and the Islamic world, where it gained popularity and underwent significant rule changes. The game was known as "Shatranj" during this period.

Medieval Europe

Chess reached Europe through the Moors in Spain. The rules were further refined, and the modern chess pieces as we know them today were established.

Modern Chess

In the 19th century, chess underwent a revolution with the introduction of new strategies and opening theories. The first official World Chess Championship was held in 1886.

Chess Today

Chess is now a globally popular game, played by millions of people of all ages. It is recognized as a sport and has its own international governing body, FIDE.
